Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Spendly takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the personal information you provide when using our expense tracking application.

Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your email address and a password (stored securely in hashed form). As you use Spendly, we store the expense data you enter — including amounts, categories, descriptions, and dates — as well as any profile information you choose to provide. We do not collect any sensitive financial information such as bank account numbers, credit card numbers, or transaction identifiers.

How We Use Your Data

The data you enter is used exclusively to power Spendly's features: displaying your expenses, generating summaries and spending breakdowns, and allowing you to filter or search your transaction history. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers and is accessible only to you through your account. We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, alteration, or loss. You can delete your data at any time by removing individual expenses or closing your account.

Third Party Services

Spendly does not integrate with third-party services that would receive your personal data. We do not use analytics trackers, advertising networks, or social media plugins. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and notify you accordingly.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app.
